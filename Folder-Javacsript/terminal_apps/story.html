Moral: Honesty is always rewarded.
Once there lived a poor woodcutter near a river.
He went to cut wood in the forest and worked hard every day.
The river was deep. Suddenly, his axe fell into the water!
He sat on the bank and cried. Then Mercury appeared in shining light.
Mercury asked why he was crying. The woodcutter told the whole truth.
Mercury brought a golden axe, but the woodcutter refused it.
Then Mercury brought a silver axe, but he refused that one too.
At last, Mercury brought the iron axe. The woodcutter smiled and took his own axe.
Honesty is always rewarded.
Mercury was pleased. He gave back the golden and silver axes too.
