Honesty Story
Mood: Happy
Lesson: Honesty
Moral: Honesty is the best policy.
A happy boy named Ali lived in a small village.
$
One day, he found some money on the road.
Ali was honest, so he returned the money to the old man.
The old man smiled and thanked him with gifts and blessings.
Honesty is the best policy.
Ali became known in the village as a good and honest boy.
